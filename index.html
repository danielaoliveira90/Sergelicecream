My favorite ice cream: Sorvetes Sergel - Brazil

About Sorvetes Sergel

Sergel Ice Cream is a beloved and traditional ice cream parlor in my hometown of Campinas, São Paulo, Brazil. With eight locations, over 100 different products, and a customer base of more than 50,000, Sergel has become a local favorite.
Since 1982, this family-owned business has been crafting artisanal ice creams of the highest quality at fair prices. Sergel offers a wide variety of flavors to satisfy every taste, making it a cherished part of our community.

A Delicious Range of Sergel Products

Sergel offers a diverse and delicious selection of treats. You can enjoy various flavors of popsicles or traditional ice cream. Some of their ice creams are water-based, while others are milk-based. The parlor crafts both classic flavors like chocolate and unique ones such as curau de milho verde, a traditional Brazilian sweet turned into ice cream.
Some of the menu highlights include peanut, hazelnut cream with cocoa, white coconut, toasted coconut, condensed milk, chocolate chip, mint, strawberry, pistachio with cashew nuts, lemon pie, açaí, pineapple, cajá, tangerine, lemon, passion fruit, grape, chocolate with cookies, and rum raisin. With so many flavors, a trip to Brazil is the only way to fully experience and fall in love with Sergel's creations!
